What is SAP S/4HANA?

SAP S/4HANA is a next-generation enterprise resource planning (ERP) system designed for large businesses. It's the successor to SAP ECC (Enterprise Central Component) and offers several key improvements:

  • Make real-time decisions with in-memory processing.
  • Work smarter with a streamlined data model.
  • Enjoy a user-friendly interface for better adoption.
  • Stay ahead of the curve with future-proof technology.
